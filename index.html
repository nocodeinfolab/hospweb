Service Procedures & Patient Preparation Guidelines

MRI

  • Arrive 30 min before appointment
  • No metal objects, fasting may be required
  • Inform staff of implants or claustrophobia

CT Scan

  • Bring previous reports if any
  • Fasting may be needed for contrast studies
  • Remove jewelry and metallic items

Blood Test

  • Fasting 8-12 hours for some tests
  • Stay hydrated unless otherwise advised
  • Follow instructions for medication use

Ultrasound

  • Drink water for pelvic scans
  • Do not urinate before abdominal scans
  • No special prep for other types

Laboratory Capabilities

  • Comprehensive blood, urine, and tissue analysis
  • Microbiology culture and sensitivity
  • Molecular diagnostics including PCR
  • Rapid turnaround for stat/emergency cases

Imaging Services & Radiology Procedures

  • Digital X-ray (chest, bone, dental, etc.)
  • MRI (brain, spine, joints, abdomen)
  • CT scans (trauma, angiography, cancer screening)
  • Ultrasound (obstetric, abdominal, vascular)
  • Interventional radiology (biopsies, drainages)
